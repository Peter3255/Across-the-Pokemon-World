I Have Seen it All - and I want to See More!

My Pokémon pals and I with the sea. New adventures are beyond the ocean. Note if the author's face looks more red than usual, overall looks more blury, it might be the display tone you set your computer on, or screen size you're viewing the picture. Also I didn't realize until later the dark quality picture I took of myself...

I have been all over the Pokémon world. I have discovered ancient lore that, when compiled together and connecting its entire meaning, makes me think of the Pokémon world fondly, sentimentally, and a blessing.

I have inspired a trainer to find hope and enthusiasm in helping the ones she cares so much for. I have made friends with Pokémon I like to hang around with, and grew an increased fondness to them through battles. So many Pokémon that make me smile, brighten my day, and am thankful they are my partners.

When the world was at stake, like the legend of the heroes and the legendary Pokémon, I summoned the legendary Pokémon and fought alongside to take on the Pokémon threatening Galar. With the legendary Pokémon and Hop by my side, I took on the role of a hero at that battle against the colossal, noxious and invincible-looking dragon.

I have seen nature that is vivid, and beautiful and looks like it is in harmony with the music of orchestral thrill, and dreams reached. I have seen the legendary Pokémon, the magnificent, rainbow bird that is sighted by a pure-hearted person, brings happiness, and is very rarely spotted flying in the sky.


And I have witnessed many more of the world. Even through all those adventures - I want to see more!



Excuse the picture if you think it is badly made. If so, I hope you find it exciting for what it is.

The experiences I choose to adventure in the world of Pokémon, will be vastly different from others inhabiting the incredible and established universe.

Out there there are countless uncharted stories to be forged. All containing potential for many different journeys to go on.




The world makes me so happy that no matter how much I go through in my personal life, I will get reminded I can pass through those times, forget about it, and smile.

And in the Pokémon world, even if there are fellow trainers that need my help to save the day, I like to think I can still bring that personality with me.


The stories inspire me to always keep my chin up and battle on to help fellow trainers and characters in the world. In those times, hanging in there, and prevailing is an exciting experience to immerse in.



What I Desire in the Future

Everything in the universe is amazing. I am grateful for it.

There are still many adventures that have yet to unfold. And I want to travel the world endlessly and always feel the joy of the world in new ways!


Happy Pokémon 28th anniversary.
