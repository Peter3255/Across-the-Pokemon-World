Across the Pokémon World is what I call a variety website, focused on Pokémon, and other games I like. The site has a diverse range of fun and exciting content. Content can be in the form of articles, videos, pictures, and now quizzes and polls! While I may be busy with other obligations in life, I aim to release all those forms of media or at least an article, video, quiz and poll every two weeks.




Most of the articles on the site are themed around Pokémon, but I would like to write about games other than the series or gaming in general. The first two generations of Pokémon games, to me, though containing simple, old, visuals, give a feeling like I'm adventuring, wanting to know about these creatures from ancient myths and stories, becoming friends with them, taking on a role of a hero, and aiming to be a champion, as if of a sport. It's all an adventure a child would love to go on. Pokémon is like some kind of grand journey. It's like a child's fantasy.

This is, what Pokémon is to me.

Other games would later be made in the franchise, and bring even more exciting experiences.

My goal for the articles is to seek and discover the magic, wonder, stories and dreams of this amazing world, and tell its many stories and experiences the player can take part and interact with. But I would like to write about other types of Pokémon articles as well.


I'm starting to do time trial challenge videos for the website. I want to release these videos every two weeks. And if possible, I would like to deliver a duo of gameplay clips alongside the challenge videos!
